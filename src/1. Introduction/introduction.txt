> What Are Design Patterns ?
    Design Patterns are effective solutions to 
    common application development challenges

<> A Caveat 
    _ The design patterns we talk about in this course are not the 
        gang of four OOP design patterns

    _ The patterns we cover here are effective
        solutions to some extrememly common challenges in React

<> Common challenges    
    _ creating reusanle layouts
    _ reusing complex logic between multiple components
    _ working with forms
    _ incorporating functional concepts into our code
